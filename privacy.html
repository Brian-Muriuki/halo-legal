Introduction

Welcome to Halo. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and meaningful spiritual experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Halo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Profile picture (optional)

Spiritual Content You Create:

  • Journal entries (private and secure)
  • Prayer requests (public or private, as you designate)
  • Testimonies (public)
  • Comments and replies
  • Circle (faith community) posts and messages

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • IP address and general location (city/country level)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: Create and maintain your account, provide daily spiritual content, enable community features
  • To Personalize: Recommend relevant content and customize your experience
  • For Safety: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, and protect our community
  • For Communication: Send important updates and respond to support requests
  • For Analytics: Understand usage patterns and improve the app

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Public Information

YOU control what's public:

  • Prayer requests marked as "public" are visible to all users
  • Testimonies are public by default
  • Your profile information (name, picture) is visible to other users
  • Circle posts are visible to circle members

Private by Default:

  • Journal entries are NEVER shared (protected by Row Level Security)
  • Private prayer requests are only visible to you
  • Your email address is NEVER shown to other users

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (SOC 2 certified)
  • OpenAI: AI content generation (does NOT receive personal data)
  • Expo/OneSignal: Push notifications

4. Data Security

How we protect your information:

  • Encryption: TLS/SSL for all data transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access, multi-factor authentication
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on secure, SOC 2 certified servers

Data Retention:

  • Active accounts: Information retained while account is active
  • Deleted accounts: Permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized data: May be retained indefinitely for analytics

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data (Settings → Privacy → Download My Data)
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information (Settings → Profile)
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
  • Export: Download all your data in JSON format
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics (Settings → Privacy → Analytics)

GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights including data portability, restriction of processing, and the right to object.

CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have rights to know what data we collect, delete data, and opt-out of data sales. We do NOT sell your personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

Halo is NOT intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. By using Halo, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection laws.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification and email.
